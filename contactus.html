Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our publishing process.

How long does it take to publish a book?

The timeline varies depending on the manuscript's status and the services required. Generally, the process takes between 6 to 12 months from manuscript acceptance to the final release.

Do you accept unsolicited manuscripts?

Yes, we welcome submissions from new and established authors. Please review our submission guidelines before sending your manuscript to ensure it meets our requirements.

What genres do you specialize in?

We specialize in Fiction (Sci-Fi, Fantasy, Thriller), Non-Fiction (Self-Help, Biography), and Children's Literature. However, we are always open to unique and compelling stories across all genres.

Do you offer editing services?

Yes, we provide comprehensive editing services including developmental editing, copy editing, and proofreading to ensure your manuscript is polished to perfection.

How can I distribute my book internationally?

We work with major global distributors and retailers to ensure your book reaches readers worldwide, available in both print and digital formats across multiple platforms.
